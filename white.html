**white/off-white** Spring 2020 white/off-white was a collaborative effort between me, a sociology student, and 4 architecture students for the 2020 Rice Architecture Archi- Arts Charette. We were tasked with creating an interactive site-specific installation that responded to the event's theme, Duo. Our work was inspired by a definition of duo as *two complete wholes working in harmony*. We explored this interpretation by dividing the installation into two unique spaces with a suspended white & off-white checkered sheet. This created two dual viewing experiences (outside, above, below, inside). The two colors were chosen because the subtlety of duality â€” the two colors are indistinguishable until brought together.
