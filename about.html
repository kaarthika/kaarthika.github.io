This is the personal website created by kaarthika thakker using HTML/CSS, last updated Feb. 21, 2021. The source code can be found on my github here.

kaarthika is a writer, artist, and organizer living in occupied Lenapehoking (Astoria, Queens). She spends her days using digital communications to fight for material wins for working-class NYers and organizing with our neighbors to build power in our neighborhood. She is also currently exploring stand up comedy, building a yoga practice, and birdwatching.

This page is both a final product and a work in progress as I further explore our relationship to code, screens, each other, and the world.
