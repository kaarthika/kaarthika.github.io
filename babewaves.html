babewaves

I re-started babewaves in 2018 on ktru (96.1FM) in response to play less men on our radio station. Babewaves is a genre-spanning radio hour consisting of music made by women, femme, trans, and nonbinary artists.

After a year of DJing the show on my own, I introduced a rotating set of DJs. The show still continues at wednesdays at 10 pm CT on ktru.org.
