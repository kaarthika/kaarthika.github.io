look for america

Cameron and I djed Look For America on freshair.radio in Edinburgh. We explored local music & diy scenes of different U.S. regions where each show was a different part of our audio roadtrip. At this point, I had spent most of my time in Ohio and Texas, and Cameron in Arkansas and Colorado. The show was a way to share about ourselves, our friends' music, and share American diy music with a U.K. audience. You can listen here.

I brought this show back to Houston, where Alec and I DJed Look for America on KTRU 96.1FM. The show still continues on ktru, but is now called the Road Show.
