Look for America explores the local & diy music scenes of American cities in a "road trip style" (each show is a stop that focuses on a new city). I started Look for America with my co-dj at University of Edinburgh's online radio station freshair.org.uk (shows here) and continued it in Houston at KTRU on-air with another DJ. is a on-air radio show (ktru.org / 96.1 FM in Houston) .
